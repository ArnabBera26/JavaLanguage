throw (Used to manually throw an exception)
    throw is used inside a method or block.
    It manually throws an exception.
    Can be used with both checked and unchecked exceptions.

throws (Used in method signature to declare exceptions)
    throws is used in the method declaration to indicate that the method might throw exceptions.
    It does not throw an exception itself, but alerts the caller that exceptions may occur.
    Mostly used for checked exceptions like IOException or SQLException.
